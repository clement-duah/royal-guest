Explore Our Property

Take a closer look at what makes Royal Maricay Hotel special

🏢 Hotel Exterior

Our beautifully designed hotel building features modern architecture with traditional Ghanaian design elements. The well-maintained exterior, lush gardens, and secure parking facilities create a welcoming first impression.

🛏️ Comfortable Rooms

From cozy Standard rooms to luxurious Executive suites, every room is designed with your comfort in mind. Each room features modern furnishings, quality bedding, air conditioning, and all necessary amenities for a pleasant stay.

🍽️ Dining & Restaurant

Our on-site restaurant serves delicious local and international cuisine prepared by skilled chefs. The comfortable dining areas and attentive service ensure a memorable dining experience for all our guests.

🏋️ Modern Facilities

We provide excellent facilities including conference rooms for business meetings, fitness areas, lounge spaces for relaxation, and other amenities designed to make your stay comfortable and productive.
