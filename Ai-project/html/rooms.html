Cancellations made up to 48 hours before check-in date are eligible for a full refund. Cancellations within 48 hours may incur charges.

We accept cash (GHS, USD, EUR), mobile money, bank transfers, and card payments. Payment is due upon check-in unless other arrangements are made.

Children stay free with parents. Pets are not allowed in our rooms but we provide alternative arrangements upon request.
