About Royal Maricay Hotel

Discover our story, values, and commitment to hospitality

Welcome to Royal Maricay Hotel

Royal Maricay Hotel is a welcoming guest house located in the heart of Tanoso, Ghana. For years, we have been dedicated to providing exceptional accommodation and hospitality services to travelers, business professionals, families, and tourists from around the world.

Positioned conveniently opposite the Tanoso Community Nursing School, our hotel serves as a home away from home for nursing students' families, business travelers, and visitors exploring the vibrant community of Tanoso.

We believe that hospitality is more than just providing a room—it's about creating memorable experiences, building lasting relationships, and ensuring every guest leaves with a warm heart and a desire to return.
